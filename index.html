Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Transfer to Chandigarh

Today arrival at Chandigarh airport/Railway Station and transfer to the Hotel. Overnight stay at Chandigarh.
Pick up Time: 05:00 PM Delhi Airport

Day 2

Chandigarh to Narkanda

Today morning after breakfast get transfer to Narkanda on the way Visit to Shimla mall road, green valley, kufri . Overnight stay at Narkanda.

Day 3

Narkanda to Kalpa

Today morning after breakfast checkout and get transfer to kalpa Overnight stay will be in Kalpa. You will enter Kinnaur district, we will drive to ReoKong Peo main town of Kinnaur district. We will spend the night in the beautiful Kalpa village overlooking Kinnaur Kailash Mountains.

Day 4

Kalpa Town to Tabo Village

Labeled as world’s most treacherous roads on road signs by BRO or Border Road Organization, you can find the boards along the road and take a selfie. Roads will be winding and high with extreme valley exposure and some of the most beautiful you ever been. Keep hydration level high today as you will be gaining height today. You will be passing Nako loops, Nako Lake, Nako monastery; after Sumdo check post we finally enter Spiti Valley. We will halt for the night at Tabo Village.

Day 5

Drive to Kaza - Kye Monastery - Kibber

Kaza is the main town Spiti valley on the banks of Spiti River. Indian oil operates here India's highest petrol pump ASL 12,250 feet. You can only refill fuel here. Kye monastery is on top of a mountain overlooking the Spiti valley and a vast river bed. Kye monastery is the most famous monastery in Spiti Valley among tourist. Kibber Village was once the highest motorable village in the world. It is famous for wildlife and amazing narrow valley hugging roads.

Day 6

Langza - Hikkim - Komic (World's Highest Location)

We will drive to Langza famous for fossils found around the village and excellent for Astrophotography. Hikkim Village has the highest post office in the world. Rinchen Chhering runs it since its inception in 1983. You can buy a postcard here and post it to your friends. It comes with a stamp saying from the highest post office in the world. Komic is now the highest motorable road in the world.

Day 7

Kaza to Chandratal Lake

We will drive to Losar the last village of Spiti valley then Kunzum Pass to Chandratal Lake. Lake is famous for pristine beauty; it is a sacred place. Overnight stay in camp Chandratal. The lake of the moon located at ASL 14,100 feet popular for camping.

Day 8

Chandertal to Manali

The final long stretch from Chandratal to Manali is the most beautiful part of the Road Trip. Surrounded by Mountains, crossing glacier feed rivers at Chatru, beautiful meadows. The road on this route will be unpaved mostly and difficult to drive; we will make slow progress. It is one of the most scenic drives in India.

Day 9

Board Flight to Return (Chandigarh)

Leave early in the morning to catch your evening flight/train/ bus from Chandigarh back to your hometown. The trip ends here.
