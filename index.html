Day-wise Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Welcome to Chandigarh

Your adventure begins! Arrive at Chandigarh Airport or Railway Station, where our team will warmly welcome you. We’ll transfer you to a comfortable hotel to relax and prepare for the epic journey ahead.
Pick up Time: 05:00 PM Delhi Airport

Day 2

The Ascent Begins: Chandigarh to Narkanda

Enjoy a hearty breakfast before we trade the city constraints for the majestic mountains. En route to the serene town of Narkanda, we'll explore Shimla’s iconic Mall Road, the lush Green Valley, and charming Kufri. Unwind with an overnight stay in the peaceful embrace of Narkanda.

Day 3

Entering the Kinnaur Valley: Narkanda to Kalpa

Wake up to crisp mountain air! Today’s drive takes us deep into the enchanting Kinnaur district. We’ll pass through Recong Peo before arriving at the breathtaking Kalpa village. Spend the evening mesmerized by the panoramic views of the sacred Kinnaur Kailash Mountain range right from your stay.

Day 4

The Gateway to Spiti: Kalpa to Tabo

Brace yourself for one of the world's most thrilling and spectacular drives! Navigate the legendary winding roads built by the BRO—perfect for an adventurous selfie. As we gain altitude (stay hydrated and energized!), witness the dramatic landscape shift. We'll conquer the famous Nako Loops, visit the tranquil Nako Lake and Monastery, and finally cross the Sumdo check post to enter the magical Spiti Valley. Overnight stay in the ancient village of Tabo.

Day 5

Heart of Spiti: Kaza, Kye Monastery & Kibber

Today we venture to Kaza, the vibrant heart of Spiti Valley. Stand in awe of the iconic Kye Monastery, perched dramatically atop a mountain overlooking the vast river bed. Later, explore Kibber, deeply famous for its rich wildlife sightings and incredible narrow gorge roads. It's a day of profound cultural and visual wonders.

Day 6

Reaching the Clouds: Langza, Hikkim & Komic

Experience the exhilaration of the world's highest habitats! Discover ancient marine fossils in Langza (a paradise for astrophotography). Mail a heartfelt postcard directly from Hikkim—the world's highest post office. Finally, celebrate the achievement of reaching Komic, the highest motorable village on Earth.

Day 7

The Lake of the Moon: Kaza to Chandratal

Prepare for unparalleled natural beauty. We’ll journey through Losar and cross the breathtaking Kunzum Pass to reach our destination: Chandratal Lake. Nestled at 14,100 feet, this sacred, crescent-shaped lake boasts pristine, crystal-clear waters. Experience the ultimate adventure of camping under a sky full of stars. Overnight stay at Chandratal Camp.

Day 8

The Epic Descent: Chandratal to Manali

Today is arguably the most visually stunning stretch of the entire road trip. We embark on a rugged drive through towering mountains, glacier-fed rivers at Chatru, and vibrant green meadows. Though the terrain is unpaved and progress slow, the raw, unfiltered scenery makes it one of the most magnificent drives in all of India. Relax with an overnight stay in Manali.

Day 9

Journey Home: Manali to Chandigarh

After an incredible, life-changing expedition filled with new friendships and unforgettable memories, we’ll depart early for Chandigarh. We'll drop you at the airport/railway station for your onward journey home, carrying a piece of Spiti in your heart.
